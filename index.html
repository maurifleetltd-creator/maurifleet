Our journey — built on trust
Founded in the early 2000s by Mrs. Sobha, Maurifleet started with a single truck and a vision: to offer unmatched reliability. Through dedication, word-of-mouth recommendations, and genuine customer relationships, we steadily climbed the ladder. Each satisfied client became a pillar of our reputation. Today, we stand as a symbol of trust, punctuality, and integrity in the logistics sector of Mauritius. Our growth is fueled by real partnerships, not just contracts.
